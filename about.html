Our Story

Built on Vision,
Grounded in Trust

Megha Mythri Projects LLP was founded with a singular vision — to make premium, legally sound real estate accessible to every aspiring homeowner and investor in Hyderabad.

Under the leadership of Chairman & CEO M. Prabhakar, we have grown from a passionate startup to one of Hyderabad's most trusted real estate brands. Our projects span Shadnagar, Yacharam, Bibinagar, Adibatla, and beyond — each carrying full HMDA, DTCP, TG RERA or FCDA approvals.

We believe real estate is not just a transaction — it's a life decision. That's why we walk every client through the entire journey, from plot selection to registration, with complete transparency and personal care.
